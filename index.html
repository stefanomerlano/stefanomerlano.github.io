About

Blithe Rocher is an engineering manager based in Oakland, California. She works for Fastly as part of the core engineering team to build customer-facing configuration UIs and APIs. Prior to becoming a developer, she received a PhD in physical chemistry from the University of Southern California.

Additionally, Blithe is passionate about teaching other women how to become developers. She helped organize Rails Girls ATL, a monthly meetup for women interested in learning about programming.

When she's not crafting web applications, she spends time traveling, riding her bike, or with her dog Nola.
